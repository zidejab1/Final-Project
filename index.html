Get to Know Zideja!

Hi! My name is Zideja Bowie and I am a student at the University of Advancing Technology! I am majoring in Business Technology, Vice President of Art club, and I am also a Resident Assistant at UAT's Founder's Hall!Outside of school, I spend my time doing a multitude of things such as reading, making music, crocheting and so much more! Though school and work keep me pretty busy, I adore sight seeing and traveling!


Arizona has been my home for 11 years, before creating a life here, I traveled the world as I grew up a military child. Growing up, I was given the opportunity to live in many beautiful places such as Germany, England, France, and the beautiful state of Arizona!I can confidentally say that Arizona has been my favorite place to live.
